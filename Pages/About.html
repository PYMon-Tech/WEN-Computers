About Us

WEN computers has been lasted for more than 10 years. Since 2008, our company has been delivering the best gaming systems for most of the gamers around the world. We have fueled the passion for gaming by giving our customers the highest quality pre-built gaming computers. Our company has been the backbone of most professional gamers, game developers and everyday consumers.

For now, our office is only in Glasgow, Kentucky, UK but we are trying to open new branches all over the world as most of our regular customers has been requested. We also participate in helping the charity, donating the medicine and food for the people wo have suffered from natural disasters and now we have our foundation group.
